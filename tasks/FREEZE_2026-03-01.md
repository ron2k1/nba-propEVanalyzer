### Backtest Interpretation

- Freeze GO/NO-GO should rely on forward paper-trading (`paper_summary`), not
  on legacy historical headline metrics.
- Local backtests (`nba_mod.py backtest ... --local --odds-source local_history`)
  use `LocalNBAStats` and avoid the NBA API season-leakage path.
- Older-period NBA API backtests remained unsafe until season-parameter
  refactors were completed post-freeze.

**GO-LIVE gate** (`paper_summary` -> `gate.gatePass`):
- `sample >= 30`
- `roi > 0.0`
- `positive_clv_pct >= 50.0`
- No stat with `>= 20` signals and hit rate `< 45%`

`book=user_supplied` entries so freeze scorecards reflected automation-only
results.
