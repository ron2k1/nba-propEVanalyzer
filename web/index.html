Prop EV

Evaluate a single player prop. Enter the player, matchup, stat, line, and odds to get the model's projection, probability distribution, and EV verdict. Auto Sweep queries all sportsbooks for the best available line on that player/stat combo. The model uses Normal CDF for most stats and Poisson for steals, blocks, 3PM, and turnovers.

Live Projection

Track a player during a live game. Pulls the real-time boxscore and blends current production with remaining-minutes pace to project their final stat line. The pace bar shows how far through their expected minutes they are.

Parlay EV

Calculate the combined EV of a 2-3 leg parlay. Accounts for correlation between legs on the same game. Edit the JSON legs array below with each leg's player, stat, line, side, and odds. Includes Kelly criterion sizing.
